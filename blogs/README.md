trains engineering judgment. Written for Medium / LinkedIn. Each post stands alone, but they
build on each other: the *why*, the *how*, and the *discipline* behind the build.

## Case-study series (`/case-studies`)

A second, standalone series built on the two **golden examples** that ship in the app
(load them via *Settings → Load golden examples*, then read the artifacts as you read the
posts). Where the series above explains *how EJOS was built*, this one shows *EJOS in use* —
the strongest "watch me reason" hiring signal.

| # | Post | Angle | Best for |
|---|------|-------|----------|

Each case-study post maps 1:1 to artifacts a reader can load and explore in the app, so the
writing and the product reinforce each other.

## Suggested publishing order

Post **01** first (it sets up the problem and hooks a general audience), then alternate
between depth posts. **02** and **03** are the strongest pure-technical pieces; **04** and
**05** are the strongest "how a senior engineer works" signals — lead with those on LinkedIn
if the goal is recruiter/peer visibility.
